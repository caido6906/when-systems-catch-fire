# Terminology

## Dianhuo

The moment when a new order is not only built but recognized.

## Building the house

Constructing the collaboration system.

## Lighting the fire

Activating the recognition system.

## Proposer

The side that offers the new system.

## Responder

The side that must decide whether to enter or maintain the new system.

## Exit right

The pressure condition under which responder commitment is tested.
