## 函数内容

P_collision(WM) = P_slot × P_priority × P_overlap **三个乘法因子：** P_slot = 1 - N_active/WM - 槽位可用率，随WM↑ - 缓存越大，越容易装下信息 P_priority = σ(α_pri × N_active/WM - θ_pri)
