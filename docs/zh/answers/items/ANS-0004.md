## 问题 / Question

中文：秦统一为什么很快就崩了？
English: Why did Qin's unification collapse so quickly?

## 新答案 / New Answer

中文：秦的问题不在于统一硬件不足，而在于把退出权压缩成了象征层，缺少缓冲与真实认同。
English: Qin's problem was not insufficient unifying hardware; it compressed exit rights into a symbolic layer and lacked buffering and real identity.

English: The novelty is to read success or failure through the gradient of exit rights: forced unification without a buffer pushes a system to a brittle point.
