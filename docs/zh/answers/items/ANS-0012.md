## 问题 / Question

中文：为什么宇宙整体的波函数对内部观测者总是不可完全知道？
English: Why is the universe's overall wavefunction never fully knowable to observers inside it?

## 新答案 / New Answer

中文：因为观测者永远是宇宙的子系统，子系统对超系统的门控和维度压缩必然不对称，所以完整状态不可达。
English: Because observers are always subsystems of the universe, and gating plus dimensional compression from subsystem to supersystem is necessarily asymmetric, so the full state is unreachable.
