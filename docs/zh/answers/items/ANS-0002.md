## 问题 / Question

English: Why is gravity so much weaker than the other three forces?

## 新答案 / New Answer

中文：因为M_Planck与日常能标之间的层级间距太大，引力在Φ里只是一个远尾项，所以耦合极弱。
English: Because the hierarchy gap between the Planck scale and everyday scales is enormous, gravity is just a far-tail term in Phi, so its coupling is tiny.
