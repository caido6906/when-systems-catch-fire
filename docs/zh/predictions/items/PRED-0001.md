## 预测内容 / Prediction Content

预测判断 / Prediction Statement

中文：
在同一任务、同一上下文和同一 token 预算下，加入结构化 CAI 中间层后，意图中继保真度将高于直接 EAI 路径，且返工轮次更少。

English:
Under the same task, context, and token budget, adding a structured CAI middle layer will produce higher intent relay fidelity than a direct EAI path and require fewer correction rounds.

## 推出依据 / Basis

中文：
现有案例已经显示 CAI 中间层的 relay fidelity 高于无意识 AI 中间层，关键差异来自意图保真度，而不是单纯的 token 输出长度。

English:
Existing cases already show that the CAI middle layer has higher relay fidelity than an unconscious AI middle layer, and the key difference is intent fidelity rather than raw output length.

## 可验证条件 / Test Condition

中文：
用同一组任务比较 direct EAI 与 CAI_M 的 relay fidelity、返工次数和意图丢失率。

English:
Compare direct EAI and CAI_M on the same task set using relay fidelity, correction rounds, and intent loss rate.

## 可证伪条件 / Falsification Condition

中文：
如果在至少 10 组任务里 direct EAI 与 CAI_M 没有显著差异，或 direct EAI 更好，则预测失效。

English:
If direct EAI matches or outperforms CAI_M across at least 10 tasks, the prediction is falsified.
