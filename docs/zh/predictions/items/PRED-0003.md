## 预测内容 / Prediction Content

预测判断 / Prediction Statement

中文：
在改革或新系统采纳场景里，单纯降低经济成本不会显著改变采纳速度；只有身份和社会退出成本下降时，系统才会明显进入第 5 步或接近第 5 步。

English:
In reform or adoption settings, lowering economic cost alone will not materially change adoption speed; only when identity and social exit costs fall will the system move into Step 5 or near it.

## 推出依据 / Basis

中文：
退出权函数 A3 / A5 / A7 / A9 与多条历史案例都显示，真正锁定系统的不是单一金钱成本，而是身份、社会关系与感知退出权。

English:
Exit-right functions A3/A5/A7/A9 and multiple historical cases show that the true lock-in mechanism is not money alone but identity, social ties, and perceived exit right.

## 可验证条件 / Test Condition

中文：
比较仅有经济补贴与同时降低身份/社会退出成本的两个改革场景，记录采用率、留存率和反弹率。

English:
Compare a reform scenario with only economic subsidy against one that also lowers identity/social exit costs, and measure adoption, retention, and rebound rate.

## 可证伪条件 / Falsification Condition

中文：
如果只改经济成本就能稳定提升采纳速度，而身份/社会退出成本变化不明显，则预测失效。

English:
If changing only economic cost can reliably improve adoption speed while identity/social exit costs remain unchanged, the prediction is falsified.
